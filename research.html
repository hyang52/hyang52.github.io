Research interests

  • Numerical partial differential equations
  • PDE-constrained optimization / inverse problems
  • Reduced order modeling
  • Boundary integral equations
  • High performance computing for engineering problems: computational fluid dynamics, cardiac electrophysiology, acoustic modeling, etc.
