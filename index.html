Huanhuan Yang

I am a postdoc in the Department of Scientific Computing at Florida State University .
I am working in the group of Max Gunzburger on applied Mathematics.
Particular areas: Stochastic optimal control, Parallel reduced order modeling, Fast CVT grid generation, Ocean modeling.

I obtained my PhD degree in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at Emory University, supervised by Alessandro Veneziani on computational Mathematics.
Particular areas: Inverse problems in cardiac electrophysiology, Reduced order modeling.
