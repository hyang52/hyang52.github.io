<h1>Huanhuan Yang</h1>
<font size="3">
<p>I am an associate professor in the <a href="https://math.stu.edu.cn/Default.aspx">Department
 of Mathematics</a> at Shantou University, China.

<p>Before my current position, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the <a href="https://www.sc.fsu.edu/">Department of Scientific Computing</a> 
at Florida State University, working with <a href="https://www.sc.fsu.edu/people?uid=mgunzburger">Max 
    Gunzburger</a> on applied Mathematics.<br>
<em><b>Particular areas</b></em>: stochastic optimal control, ocean modeling.

<p>I obtained my PhD degree in the <a href="http://www.mathcs.emory.edu/site/home/">Department of Mathematics 
and Computer Science</a> at Emory University, supervised by <a href="http://www.mathcs.emory.edu/~ale/">
    Alessandro Veneziani</a> on computational Mathematics. <br>
<em><b>Particular areas</b></em>: inverse problems in cardiac electrophysiology, reduced order modeling.

<p>More information about my research interests can be found on the <a href="./research.html">Research</a> tab.</p>
