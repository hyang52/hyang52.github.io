Huanhuan Yang

I am an associate professor in the Department of Mathematics at Shantou University, China.

Before my current position, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Scientific Computing at Florida State University, working with Max Gunzburger on applied Mathematics.
Particular areas: PDE constrained optimization, parallel computing, ocean modeling.

I obtained my PhD degree in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at Emory University, supervised by Alessandro Veneziani on computational Mathematics.
Particular areas: computational electrocardiology, inverse problems, reduced order modeling.

More information about my research interests can be found on the Research tab.
